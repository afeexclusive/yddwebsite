About YDD

An Army of Young people going to Heaven and Presuading others to come along.

I’d rather love Him for Who He is. Who is He (God)? God is love And we have known and believed the love that God hath to us. God is love; (1 John 4:16a). It is because of His love for us that He thought of us in our fallen and doomed state, He came to rescue us thereby offering us salvation for free.

Because of His Love for us, we have been adopted as His Chlidren (Galatians 4:5-7), He has put His Holy spirit in us and given us power over darkness in all forms. So it is not all about you; It’s all about glorifying Him, worshipping Him and genuinely loving Him for who He is irrespective of whether or not He does things for you.
